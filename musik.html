Notes

  • Original piano pieces were composed on the fly, mostly improvised, and never notated.
  • Electronic pieces are inspired by the planetary orbital elements.
  • Renditions are personal interpretations, not faithful transcriptions.
  • AGRICOLA (2018-2024) was a one-man band project that gave voice to different animals (mammals and birds).
    • Drums are provided by Arnaud Krakowka (non-commercial use).
    • No bass lines were introduced.
    • Animals that have collaborated include: dog (Rottweiler), sheep, cow, kiwi, chimpanzee, and grebe.
    • Extinct or unidentified animals: Broncosaurus Rex and (probably) lemur.
